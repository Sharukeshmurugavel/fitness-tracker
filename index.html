Activity Formulas

  • Walking: steps = duration * 100, distance ≈ steps * 0.0008 km
  • Jogging: steps = duration * 150, distance ≈ steps * 0.001 km
  • Cycling: distance ≈ duration * 0.3 km
  • Calories (MET-based): calories = MET * weightKg * (duration / 60)
  • Heart Rate estimate uses age-based max HR and intensity factor
